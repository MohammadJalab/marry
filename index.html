Harry Mike Wishes

Ask your kids and audience on a nostalgic journey back to the mid-1960s with "Christmas Wishes." This song incorporates the instrumentation and orchestral style of the era (although we featured an electric piano which would not have been typical)

including brushes, acoustic bass and guitar, piano, percussion, and strings. Our singers picked up the scale oriented, unison melody very quickly. Yours will too, allowing you to focus on musicality rather than intervals.
